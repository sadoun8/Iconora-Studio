Iconora Studio - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction
Iconora Studio is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect
- No telemetry by default. All core features work offline.
- Minimal information: purchase receipts (when using payment processors), support emails, and optional analytics if you opt-in.

3. Use of Information
- Payment processors handle billing data.
- Support emails used to resolve customer issues.
- Aggregated analytics (optional) used to improve product.

4. Data Retention
We retain only necessary information to provide support and for refund verification.

5. Third-Party Services
We may use Gumroad, Stripe, or FastSpring for payments. These services have their own privacy practices.

6. Security
We use reasonable measures to protect personal information, but no method is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your data by contacting business@iconora.com.
